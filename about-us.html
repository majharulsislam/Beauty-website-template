A place where you’ll

love to work

It’s easy to lose your drive and motivation when you work for a company with hectic schedules and insultingly low hourly wages.

You can of course choose to work for yourself at your or the client’s home, but it doesn’t feel secure nor convenient and you lose the benefit from the walk-in traffic professional salons have.
Opening your own salon is neither an option for many as the starting costs can easily hike above £50,000 and navigating taxes and commercial agreements are out of many people’s comfort zone.

That’s why we created Beauty Avenue, the first all-inclusive co-working space, where we take out all the inconvenience and scary from starting your own business.

Start your business

Always

Feel appreciated

At Beauty Avenue, you won’t be locked in a minimum wage job. You pay your low monthly rent plus a small percentage of your monthly net revenue and you take home most of what the clients pay for your services. As you have the chance to provide a luxury salon experience in the heart of the City of London, you can charge higher fees than you would at home or as a travelling beautician, so you can earn up to £300-400 on a busy day depending on what services do you provide.

But it’s not only money that will make you feel valued at Beauty Avenue. You will be an integral part of the salon, taking part in our bi-monthly team meetings where we all discuss our future promotions, the fortnight’s successes and failures. Everyone’s voice is heard and important to us.

We sort out the rest

You do you

At Beauty Avenue, you don’t just rent a nail station or a beauty room. We provide your personal booking system, deal with all card payments and bookings both in the salon and online. We provide you with an accountant to help you navigate your taxes and make sure that you keep as much of your earnings as possible. Our marketing team will run high-budget, widespread advertising campaigns month on month to ensure that the salon and you are always busy. When you join, we welcome you with a professional photoshoot to showcase the superstar you are and you’ll get a package of luxury business cards to give to your clients with details to our booking and reception, so our hostesses can manage your bookings for you.

Our prosecco bar will attract more clients especially for nail treatments and our drink promotions will encourage them to come to socialise in the salon, while being pampered by our professionals.
